About Us

Our restaurant is built on a love for food and community. We serve locally sourced meals crafted with care.

Welcome to Sunny Bistro, where great food meets warm hospitality! Nestled in the heart of Moldova, our restaurant offers a cozy yet sophisticated ambiance perfect for casual outings, family gatherings, or romantic dinners. We take pride in serving a menu crafted with love, featuring fresh, locally sourced ingredients that highlight both classic and contemporary flavors. From sizzling appetizers to mouthwatering main courses and indulgent desserts, every dish is a celebration of taste and quality. Whether you're here to enjoy a quick bite or a leisurely dining experience, our attentive team is dedicated to making your visit memorable. Come savor the flavors and feel at home at Sunny Bistro!
